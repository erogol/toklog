# Changelog

## 0.2.0 — 2026-03-22

Initial public release.

- **Proxy-based capture** for OpenAI, Anthropic, and Gemini traffic
- **Spend reports** with model, provider, endpoint, program, and tag breakdowns
- **Waste detection** highlighting expensive patterns worth fixing first
- **Setup wizard** (`tl proxy setup`) for guided onboarding
- **Doctor** (`tl doctor`) for diagnosing proxy and routing issues
- **Gain tracking** (`tl gain`) showing cumulative savings over time
- **Shareable HTML reports** (`tl share --open`)
- Local-only JSONL logs, no hosted backend

## 0.1.0 — internal

SDK-era prototype. Deprecated in favor of proxy-only architecture.
